Western Program Volunteer, Zhenba County Committee of Communist Youth League

Jul 2014-Jun 2016    Hanzhong, Shaanxi, China

     I participated in the Graduate Volunteer Serving Western China Program organised by the Central Committee of CYLC(Communist Youth League of China) after graduating from Northwest A&F University in July, 2014. I was assigned to do some voluntary work in Zhenba, a mountainous county that is located in southernmost part of Shaanxi Province in China. As a volunteer for serving western China program, I mainly worked in Zhenba County Committee of Communist Youth League. Guided by the local Committee of Communist Youth League, my voluntary work mainly involved in natural disaster relief, office administrative affairs, social public services, teaching assistance for local children and so on.
     In the end of my voluntary work in June of 2016, I was granted the honor of 2016 Outstanding Volunteer of the Graduate Volunteer Serving Western China Program for my contributions and efforts by Shaanxi Provincial Committee of Communist Youth League.
